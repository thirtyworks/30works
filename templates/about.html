30works30days

is a free month-long project where participating artists create and submit a new piece of work every day during April. The project is open to anyone working in any media, anywhere in the world. Artists that participate can also access peer mentoring, network support and engage with other artists taking part in the project.

The primary rule of the project is that you must submit an artwork every day. If you fail to submit an artwork on one of the days, you are out of the project.

All the information required for logging in, submitting work and navigating the site can be found below. For more information please go to the Artquest website or email us at info@thirty.works

Code of Conduct

  • Submit only content which you have created or that you have the right to share. Don't post anything you've copied or collected from the internet or that you don't have the right to post.
  • Artworks submitted which are inherently disrespectful, aggressive, or otherwise abusive will be removed, as will any artwork which targets people based of a protected characteristic such as race, ethnicity, national origin, disability, religious affiliation, sexual orientation, sex, gender identity or serious disease.
